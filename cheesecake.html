Classic Cheesecake with Strawberry Sour Cream Frosting

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cup graham crackers
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 5 tbsp melted butter
  •  
  • 4 packs (8oz) cream cheese
  • 2 (try 1) cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 5 tbsp flour
  • 3 pinches of salt
  • 1 vanilla bean
  • 4 (try 3) eggs
  •  
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla

Directions

  1. Begin with the crust by crushing the graham crackers with sugar and melted butter.
  2. use a flat item to even the crust into the pan (butter the pan and line with parchment paper around+below)
  3. Bake 8-10 minutes at 350F until golden brown, let cool.
  4. To make the cheesecake, beat cream cheese with sugar until well combined. Mix in heavy cream
  5. Mix in sour cream, flour, vanilla, and salt.
  6. Beat in ONE egg at a time. Once well combined, STOP mixing.
  7. Pour the mixture over the cooled crust. In a water bath, bake for 1 hour at 350F. Leave it in the closed oven for another hour.
  8. For the sour cream topping layer, mix together the sour cream, sugar, and vanilla.
  9. Spread it over the cheesecake and bake for 10-15 minutes at 350F (no water bath).
  10. Leave in the closed oven for 1 hour and let cool. Plastic wrap the cheesecake and let it rest overnight (at least 4 hours).
